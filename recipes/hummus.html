Hummus

Description:

Hummus is a delicious dip, made with chickpeas and Tahini. It can also be used as a spread on breat or a pita or a wrap. Hummus is also rich in protein.

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas - 1 cup
  • Tahini - 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic - 5-6 cloves
  • Lemon juice - from 1 lemon
  • Salt - 3 teaspoons
  • Black pepper - 3 teaspoons
  • Olive oil - 2-3 tablespoon
  • Water - 1-4 tablespoons (as needed)

Directions:

  1. Soak the chickpeas in water overnight. Drain the following day
  2. Boil in water for 2 hours
  3. Drain water and transfer chickpeas to a food processor
  4. Chop until very fine
  5. Add garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper and continue chopping until mixed well
  6. Add Tahini and olive oil and continue mixing in food processor until fully mixed
  7. Add water as needed until mixture is soft but not watery
  8. Enjoy!
