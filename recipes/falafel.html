Falafel

Falafe is a delicious side dish, made with chickpeas. It goes really good with Hummus or Tahini

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas - 1 cup
  • Green onions - 1/2 cup
  • Garlic - 1/3 cup
  • Parsley - 2 branches
  • Flour - 5 tablesppons
  • Salt - 2 teaspoons
  • Black pepper - 2 teaspoons
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon

Directions:

  1. Soak the chickpeas in water overnight. Drain the following day
  2. In food processor add: Green onions, garlic and pasley, chop till fine.
  3. Add the Chickpeas to foodprocessor and chop until fine
  4. Add salt, black pepper, flour and olive oil and continue chopping until all ingredients are thoroughly mixed.
  5. Make small balls (about 3/4 inch) with the mixture.
  6. Heat cooking oil to 300 degrees F.
  7. Submerge the balls in the oil and fry for 5 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
